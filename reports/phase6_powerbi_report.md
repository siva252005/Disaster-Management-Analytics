# Phase 6: Power BI Dashboard Development

## Objective

Develop interactive dashboards to analyze global disaster trends, impacts, and economic losses from 1970 to 2021.

---

# Dashboard Pages

## 1. Overview Dashboard

### KPI Cards

- Total Disasters
- Total Deaths
- Total Affected Population
- Total Economic Damage
- Total Countries

### Visualizations

- Disaster Trend (1970–2021)
- Disaster Type Distribution
- Top 10 Countries by Disaster Count
- Severity Distribution

### Filters

- Year
- Disaster Type
- Continent
- Severity

---

## 2. Impact Analysis Dashboard

### Human Impact Analysis

- Deaths by Disaster Type
- Top 10 Countries by Death Toll

### Economic Impact Analysis

- Economic Loss by Disaster Type
- Top 10 Countries by Economic Loss

### Geographical Analysis

- Global Disaster Map

### Filters

- Year
- Disaster Type
- Severity
- Continent

---

# Key Insights

- Floods are the most frequent disasters.
- Earthquakes cause the highest mortality.
- Storms account for the largest economic losses.
- Asia is the most disaster-prone region.
- China and the United States experience the most disasters.
- Bangladesh recorded the highest death toll.
- The United States experienced the highest economic losses.

---

# Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- DAX Measures
- Interactive Slicers
- Map Visualization

---

# Conclusion

The Power BI dashboards provide interactive insights into global disasters, helping understand disaster frequency, human impact, economic losses, and geographical distribution for better decision-making and preparedness.
